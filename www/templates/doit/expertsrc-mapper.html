Help us integrate Pharon assay data

Below, you will be shown pairs of data attributes.

These are mappings which have been automatically generated between assay data attributes and attributes in a global schema by an ensemble of schema matching algorithms. The matches shown below are those rated 'most similar' by our algorithms.

We need your help to determine whether these matches are correct!

What you do:

  • You will evaluate groups of 10 matches at a time. If you have been assigned more than 10 matches, then the next batch will appear when your answers have been saved.
  • Indicate whether you believe the pair is or isn't a match by pressing the or button.
    NB: You may not always be able to determine whether or not the pair is a match. This is OK. Just give your best guess and indicate how sure you are using the confidence slider that appears after you make a decision.
  • If you change your mind, you can use the button to reset the match to what we originally suggested.
    NB: After you press "Save" your answer is final!
  • You can navigate back to Expertsrc at any time by pressing the "Done" button.
  • You can view this information at any time by pressing the "Help" button. You can also mouse over the components of the mapping interface to get hints.

What you're given:

Our Score
The colored block between the assay attribute and the global attribute represents our algorithms' estimation of match strength. Green indicates a strong match and Red indicates a weak match.

Other Matches
You can select a different global attribute by pressing the button.

The Data
Press the button to get more information. You can either view the data source of the attribute being mapped or see more details about the match. (e.g., compare data values of assay and global attributes).

Caveats:

  • Note that the assay data attributes on the page might not be from the same source.
    NB: Make sure that you view the data attribute's source before making a decision!
